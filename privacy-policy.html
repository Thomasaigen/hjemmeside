MODRIĆ TOKEN PRIVACY NOTICE

Last Modified: October 12, 2025


This Modrić Token Privacy Notice (“Notice”) applies to the site at modric.trendex.gg and supplements the Trendex Platform Privacy Policy at trendex.gg/privacy (the “Platform Privacy Policy”). The Platform Privacy Policy applies in full; this Notice explains additional, token-specific handling.

This Notice also clarifies that Luka Modrić is not a data controller or processor for any personal information collected through this Site. All processing is performed by Trendex or its service providers in accordance with this Notice and the Platform Privacy Notice.

Trendex is the Personal Data Controller for all Personal Data we process in connection with this Site. For clarity, on-chain transactions are executed on decentralized networks where Trendex is not a controller and cannot alter or erase data.

By accessing or using the Site, you acknowledge and agree to this Notice. If you do not agree, please do not use the Site.

1. Data We May Collect on This Site

 1.1. Telemetry & anti-abuse: IP address, device/OS/browser, page views, timestamps, and basic diagnostics to operate the site, prevent abuse, and maintain security.

1.2. Wallet data: Public wallet addresses you connect to verify thresholds for discretionary perks/access and eligibility checks.

1.3. Perk fulfillment: If you choose to claim a physical item or in-person experience, we (or our vendors) may collect contact/shipping and verification details (e.g., name, address, email, phone; age/ID status for event access). Trendex aims to receive pass/fail outcomes rather than full documents wherever feasible.

1.4. On-ramp & payments: If you use third-party on-ramps or wallet services, collection and processing are governed by those providers’ policies. Trendex does not receive your card details or private keys.

2. How We Use Data

  • Operate and secure the site

  • Verify eligibility for discretionary perks/access

  • Deliver perks you choose to claim

  • Comply with sanctions, venue, and safety rules

  • Detect/prevent fraud or abuse

  • Perform aggregated analytics to improve user experience

  • Analyze aggregated, de-identified usage data to improve fan experiences and platform performance.


3. Sharing

We may share limited data with:

  • Fulfillment/service providers (shipping, event/venue security, verifications),

  • On-ramp/wallet partners you choose to use,

  • Hosting, analytics, and anti-fraud providers, and

  • Governmental or law enforcement authorities where legally required or permitted.

Trendex does not sell personal information and does not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

4. Security & Retention of Personal Data

We use industry-standard safeguards. While we apply industry-standard safeguards to off-chain data, you acknowledge that public blockchains are outside our control and carry inherent privacy risks. Perk-related personal data is retained only as long as needed for delivery, compliance, and audit. Such data are typically deleted within 30 days of final use. Telemetry data is minimized and rotated per our security policy. Such data are deleted within 90 days after completion or audit closure. On-chain activity is immutable, public and cannot be deleted by Trendex.

5. Your Rights

Your rights and how to exercise them (EEA/UK/California and other regions) are described in the Platform Privacy Policy. Contact: privacy@trendex.tech.

6. Children

This site is not directed to anyone under the age of 18. Access to certain perks may require age verification. We do not knowingly collect data from minors and will promptly delete such data if identified.

7. International Transfers

As described in the Platform Privacy Policy, we may process data globally in accordance with applicable law and appropriate transfer mechanisms.  If you exercise deletion rights, Trendex will delete off-chain records where possible but cannot erase public blockchain data.

8. Changes

This Notice forms part of the Trendex Platform Privacy Notice. We may update it periodically and will post the effective date. Your continued use of the Site constitutes acceptance of any updates.

Complaints and Contact : If you believe we have not complied with your privacy rights, contact us at privacy@trendex.tech. You may also contact your local data-protection authority.
